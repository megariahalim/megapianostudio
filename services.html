My services

Piano Lessons and (Group) Theory Lessons

Come and enjoy one-to-one piano lessons at Mega Piano Studio. Here in MP studio, I am committed to providing lessons and guidance for everyone. Students who wish to take exams, need help with their music elective programme, or want to learn and make music for leisure and to learn new skills are all welcome! Each lessons will be tailored to meet your needs.

Students will be building their foundations on learning notes recognition through solfeggi, rhythm and ear training. Most piano lessons in MP Studio also include theory lessons, especially for students who are preparing for graded exams. The goal is to deepen understanding and connection between the theory and the music that is created. This not only gives you greater understanding of music but will deepen your enjoyment as well. Needless to say, these are very important skills for musicians.

MP Studio usually takes 3-8 students (Grade 1-5) or 3-5 students (Grade 6-8) per class for group theory lessons. In a group class, students are always welcome and encouraged to listen and share opinions and experiences among peers, to work hard and to encourage each other, as well as to learn, appreciate, and enjoy music together with their peers.

Lessons are also available for children between the ages of 4-6. The fees is SGD $50 for 30 minutes.

Lessons are available for students who wish to prepare for graded examinations. Lessons fees starts at SGD $60. Contact MP Studio for more detailed pricing.

If you are not sure whether music lessons are for you, MP Studio offers a 20-minute trial lesson at SGD $30.

Collaborative Pianist (Accompaniment) service

Working in a chamber settings is a magical experience as you get to meet with many other like-minded musicians (who can turn in to long life friends) and to create beautiful music together. You will have the opportunity of learning from your fellow musicians as well.

Apart from piano and theory lessons, MP Studio also offers piano accompaniment services if you are looking for a pianist for your voice and/or instrumental examinations. Aural (ear) training is also included in every coaching session.

MP Studio also provides accompaniment services if you are looking for a pianist for your choir or chamber groups!

Accompaniment fees starts at $60. Prices depend on grades (if you are preparing for graded exams), events, and the timeline. Contact MP Studio for more detailed pricing.
